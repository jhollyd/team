Welcome to The VDC

The Venture Development Center (VDC) is more than just a space — it's a launchpad for tomorrow’s innovations. We provide a collaborative, inclusive environment where entrepreneurs, researchers, and changemakers come together to build impactful ventures.

Whether you’re working on breakthrough biotech, cutting-edge AI, or a disruptive startup idea, the VDC supports your journey with mentorship, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant community that believes in your vision.

Ready to turn your ideas into reality? Explore how the VDC can help you grow, connect, and thrive as part of Boston’s most exciting startup ecosystem.

About Us

Founded in 2009, the Venture Development Center at UMass Boston is a dynamic incubator for early-stage technology and life science startups. We empower entrepreneurs to bring high-impact products and solutions to the world through expert guidance and a community-driven approach.

At the VDC, members gain access to advanced labs, fully-equipped office space, experienced mentors, business services, and visa sponsorship opportunities. We bridge academia and industry, supporting both startup founders and UMass students through hands-on innovation and real-world training.

From groundbreaking research to market-ready ventures, the VDC is where ideas evolve into lasting impact.

Become a Global Entrepreneur-in-Residence

Ready to take your startup to the next level? Join the VDC as a Global Entrepreneur-in-Residence and gain access to a cap-exempt H-1B visa sponsored by UMass—available any time of the year.

As a part of our innovative program, you'll dedicate just 8 hours per week to mentoring fellow entrepreneurs, faculty, and students—while simultaneously building your company with support from our expert network.

Benefit from personalized guidance, access to office and lab space, and connections that matter. All while strengthening your case for a long-term visa through real, measurable progress.

Entrepreneurship is a journey—let the VDC be your launchpad.
