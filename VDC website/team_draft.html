Our Team

Passionate Minds, Powerful Results

Meet Our Dedicated Team of innovators, problem-solvers, and collaborators committed to driving success through creativity and expertise. Each member brings unique skills and perspectives to the table, working together to deliver exceptional outcomes and foster a culture of excellence. Get to know the people behind the projects that make our vision a reality.

Bala Sundaram

Vice-Provost for Research & Strategic Initiatives

Bala Sundaram is a theoretical physicist by training and former department chair who took up his role as the campus research officer in 2017. This role helps align campus activities within the multi-campus UMass system as well as respond to Commonwealth priorities including those in the ever-changing tech sector. The VDC helps facilitate his office’s support of the translational ambitions of both campus and regional researchers.

Shubhro Sen, as the Executive Director, leverages his global entrepreneurial background, including leading five companies to successful exits such as IPOs and buyouts. Matthew Meyer

Associate Vice Provost for Research & Director of the Office of Research & Sponsored Programs

Matthew Meyer serves as the Associate Vice Provost for Research and Director of the Office of Research and Sponsored Programs at UMass Boston. In his role, he is dedicated to enhancing the university’s research enterprise by improving research infrastructure, bolstering support services, and increasing external funding and research productivity. His efforts aim to foster a vibrant translational research and innovation culture that supports UMass Boston’s mission and academic community.
