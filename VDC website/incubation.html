Venture Incubation Program

The Venture Incubation Program is open to companies regardless of their founders' alma mater...

  • Cap-exempt H-1B visa sponsorship anytime during the year.
  • Access to a team experienced in billion-dollar companies.
  • Fully equipped lab and office space.
  • Talented interns, UMass resources, and instruments.
  • Community lunches and mentorship office hours.

Included in membership

  • Private wet/dry labs, furnished offices, shared desks.
  • Conference rooms, lounge, café, kitchen.

Included in membership

  • Shipping, hosting, printing, EHS compliance, waste removal.

Included in membership

  • 3D printer, biosafety cabinets, autoclave, centrifuges, etc.
  • Genomics, imaging, microscopy, flow cytometry, etc.

Discounted access to 90 UMass research facilities via Innovation Voucher Program.

  • 10–15 companies accepted per year; 13% acceptance rate.
  • $750 to $10,000/month depending on facility type.
  • Month-to-month flexibility.

Have questions? Please apply and we’ll reach out.
